A Letter to You

My Yarabella,

A year ago, on June 8th, 2025, at my sister's bachelorette party, I reached for a wine opener and instead found forever. That simple moment—searching for a wine opener among strangers—became the beginning of our greatest adventure.

Every day with you has been a masterpiece painted across thousands of miles. Your smile reaches me through every video call, every message, every promise we make under the same stars, just from different corners of the world.

You've taught me that distance is just a number when love is infinite. That patience is the greatest gift we can give each other. That true love doesn't measure itself in miles—it measures itself in moments, in memories, in the way my heart races every time your name lights up my phone.

Thank you for choosing us, every single day. Thank you for the wine openers and the wranglers and the road trips. Thank you for camping under stars and dancing in the rain. Thank you for Paris, for Bavaria, for every sunset we'll chase together.

We will celebrate not just the day we met, but the person you've helped me become. The love you've planted in my heart. The future we're building, one mile closer every day.

Iiiiii love you.

With all my heart,
Your Jean michou ❤️
